About Us

A Lean, Results-Driven Marketing Partner

Triune Capital Group is a digital marketing consultancy built for small businesses that need expert-level marketing without agency-level overhead. We focus on four high-impact services — SEO, copywriting, web design, and email automation — delivered as done-for-you packages with clear deliverables, transparent pricing, and measurable ROI.

Every engagement starts with understanding your business goals. We then deliver a complete, ready-to-use asset — whether that's a comprehensive SEO roadmap, a high-converting sales page, a polished new website, or an automated email system that works while you sleep.

Bundled or Standalone

Each service works on its own or can be combined (e.g. SEO audit + website build) for maximum impact and higher ROI.

Quality-Controlled

Every deliverable is reviewed for consistency. We use client feedback and A/B testing to continuously refine our methods.

Low Overhead, High Value

No bloated agency fees. We use affordable, best-in-class tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs, MailerLite) and pass the savings to you.

Our Services

Four High-Impact Service Packages

Each package is designed to deliver immediate, measurable value. Clear deliverables, transparent pricing, actionable results.

01

SEO Audit & 90-Day Roadmap

A comprehensive 20–40 page SEO audit for your website, with a prioritized 90-day action plan to improve your search rankings and organic traffic.

What's Included
  • Technical SEO review: site speed, crawl/index issues, Core Web Vitals, mobile-friendliness
  • On-page analysis: title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, internal linking, content gaps and keyword opportunities
  • Competitive analysis: identify competitors' high-ranking keywords and backlink profiles
  • Backlink audit: flag toxic links and opportunities for quality link-building
  • Prioritized 90-day roadmap of fixes and content recommendations with effort/impact estimates
  • 30–60 minute walkthrough call explaining key findings
  • Exported reports with commentary, annotated screenshots, "quick wins" list, and content gap table
$997 per audit
02

High-Converting Copywriting Package

Persuasive marketing copy for your website and email — designed around proven conversion frameworks (AIDA, PAS) to turn visitors into customers.

What's Included
  • One long-form sales page (or webinar/promo page) optimized for conversions
  • 4–5 supporting pages: Homepage, About, Services/Product, Contact — optimized for clarity and SEO
  • Optional email/sales funnel: 5–8 emails (welcome, nurture, sales, re-engagement sequences)
  • Initial strategy call to define voice, audience, and goals
  • Key message framework with headlines, subheads, benefit lists, and clear CTAs
  • One round of revisions included
  • Video walkthrough explaining copy decisions and assumptions
$2,500–$4,000 per package
03

Custom 5–8 Page Website Build

A clean, mobile-responsive website built on WordPress, Webflow, or Framer — with basic SEO, analytics, contact forms, and a training walkthrough.

What's Included
  • 5–8 pages: Home, About, Services/Products, Contact, plus 1–3 subpages
  • Modern design using your brand colors and logo with a professional template
  • Basic SEO setup: page titles, meta descriptions, Google Search Console submission
  • Google Analytics or simple analytics tool installed
  • Working contact form (emailing inquiries) and email signup integration with MailerLite
  • Mobile-friendly and speed-optimized with compression and caching
  • 5–10 min Zoom/Loom tutorial showing how to edit content, plus written "How to update" guide
  • Optional 30-day technical support for post-launch bugs
~$2,000 starter package
04

Email Marketing Automation Setup

Done-for-you automated email sequences in your chosen platform — welcome series, sales funnels, cart abandonment, and re-engagement flows that generate revenue on autopilot.

What's Included
  • 5–8 automated sequences: welcome series, lead magnet delivery, sales funnel, re-engagement / cart-abandonment flows
  • Segmentation strategy: define key subscriber segments (new leads, buyers, inactive, VIPs) with tagging
  • 10–15 email templates (HTML/text) with subject lines and CTAs matched to your brand
  • Platform setup: SPF/DKIM email authentication, form and e-commerce integrations
  • Flowchart of which sequence sends when, plus screenshots and Zoom walkthrough
  • Testing: sample sends for each flow to verify everything works
  • Step-by-step guide for adding or editing emails yourself
$1,500–$3,000 per setup
